Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 23, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the Prell's Prints Business Intelligence Dashboard ("the App") collects, uses, and handles information. This App is a private, personal business tool built and operated solely by Emily Prellwitz for the purpose of managing and analyzing her own Etsy shop and social media presence.

1. Who We Are

The App is operated by Emily Prellwitz, owner of Prell's Prints, an Etsy shop selling digital invitation templates. The App is not a public-facing product and is not available to third parties.

2. What Data We Access

The App connects to the following platforms using official APIs to access data belonging solely to the App owner:

  • Etsy API — shop performance data, order information, and active listings from the Prell's Prints Etsy shop only.
  • Pinterest API — analytics data (impressions, pin performance, board data) from the Prell's Prints Pinterest business account only.

The App does not access data belonging to any other Etsy sellers, Pinterest users, or third parties.

3. How We Use Data

Data accessed through the APIs is used exclusively to:

  • Display shop analytics and performance metrics in a private dashboard
  • Generate business insights and advertising recommendations for internal use
  • Track revenue, orders, and advertising spend over time

Data is never sold, shared, transferred, or disclosed to any third party for any purpose.

4. Data Storage

The App stores minimal data locally in the user's browser (localStorage) for session persistence, such as authentication tokens. No personal customer data from Etsy orders (including buyer names, addresses, or emails) is stored or displayed beyond what is necessary for the App owner to view their own order history.

API authentication tokens are stored securely in Vercel serverless functions and are not exposed to the public.

5. Third-Party Services

The App is hosted on Vercel and uses the following third-party APIs under their respective terms of service:

  • Etsy Open API
  • Pinterest API

The App complies with all applicable API terms of use and developer policies.

6. No Public Users

This App has no public users, no user registration, no user accounts, and no mechanism for third parties to submit data. It is a single-user private tool accessible only by the App owner.

7. Cookies

The App does not use cookies. Browser localStorage is used solely for storing the authenticated user's own session tokens.

8. Children's Privacy

This App is not directed at children and does not knowingly collect any information from anyone under the age of 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. The date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision.

10. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can reach out via the Prell's Prints Etsy shop at prellsprints.etsy.com.
